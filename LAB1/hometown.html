JINING.

Jining City, a first-level city adjacent to Shandong Province, the old capital of the State of Lu.Type II large city, Famous historical and cultural city, waterfront ecology Tourism City. Jining is located in the southwestern part of Shandong, it is a transportation hub city. Located at the Golden Section between Beijing and Shanghai. The total area of ​​the city is 11,000 square kilometers. Jining has a long history and culture, and is one of the important birthplaces of Dongyi culture, Chinese civilization, Confucian culture, Water Margin culture and canal culture. Jining is rich in cultural and tourism resources, and the "Three Holes" and the Beijing-Hangzhou Grand Canal are world cultural heritages. Jining is a big resource city. Coal reserves of more than 25 billion tons, raw coal annual output of 80 million tons, accounting for more than half of Shandong, rare earth reserves rank second in China.
